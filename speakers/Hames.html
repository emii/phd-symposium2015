Dr. Irene Hames

Research publication and peer review specialist

Irene Hames is an independent publication, and peer-review specialist with a PhD in cell biology. For 20 years, Irene was the managing editor of a large international science journal. She was a Council Member, Director and Trustee of COPE. In 2011 she was the specialist advisor to the UK Parliament for its inquiry into peer review. She is the author of the book Peer Review and Manuscript Management in Scientific Journals. She runs workshops on research integrity and publication ethics, and holds several advisory roles. In December 2011 she was made a Fellow of the Society of Biology.
